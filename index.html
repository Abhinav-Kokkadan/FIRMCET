FIRMCET

The Financial Intelligence and Risk Management Club at CET ignites financial acumen in engineering minds — turning complex market concepts into clear, actionable insights. Through workshops, quizzes, simulations, and PlaCET, we empower students to master budgeting, investing, and risk navigation with precision. Join us to cultivate the quiet confidence to shape a secure, innovative future — one informed decision at a time.
